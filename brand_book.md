# H.M. Chan & Co. Brand Identity Guide

This document outlines the brand identity for the redesigned H.M. Chan & Co. website, ensuring a consistent and professional image across all materials.

## 1. Logo

The primary logo is the white version on a dark background, and the blue version on a light background. The red accent in the logo should be the only use of that color.

- **White Logo:** `HMClogo(white).webp`
- **Blue Logo:** `HMClogo(blue).webp`

## 2. Color Palette

The color palette is designed to be professional, authoritative, and modern.

| Color          | Hex       | Usage                                        |
| :------------- | :-------- | :------------------------------------------- |
| **Primary**    | `#141b4d` | Backgrounds, primary text, dark sections     |
| **Accent**     | `#C0001A` | Logo accent, call-to-action highlights (sparingly) |
| **Secondary**  | `#FFFFFF` | Text on dark backgrounds, light sections     |
| **Tertiary**   | `#F0F0F0` | Subtle backgrounds, dividing lines           |
| **Text**       | `#333333` | Body text on light backgrounds               |

## 3. Typography

The typography is chosen for its elegance, readability, and professional feel. Both fonts are available from Google Fonts.

- **Headings:** **Lora** (Serif) - A classic and elegant serif font that conveys authority and tradition.
- **Body Text:** **Lato** (Sans-serif) - A clean, modern, and highly readable sans-serif font for all body copy.

## 4. Visual Style

The overall visual style is a blend of modern corporate and classic legal aesthetics. It should feel prestigious, trustworthy, and sophisticated.

- **Imagery:** High-quality, professional photography. A mix of Hong Kong cityscapes (especially at night), modern office interiors, and professional portraits. Images should have a slightly desaturated, moody feel to complement the color palette.
- **Iconography:** Clean, simple, and modern line icons. Icons should be used sparingly to enhance usability and visual appeal.
- **Layout:** Clean, spacious, and well-structured layouts with a strong grid system. Ample white space should be used to create a feeling of calm and focus.
