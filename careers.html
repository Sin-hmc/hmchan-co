Careers at H.M. Chan & Co.

We are firm believers in hands-on experience and are always on the lookout for talented individuals who share our commitment to excellence.

Why Join Us

Build Your Career at a Leading Boutique Firm

At H.M. Chan & Co., we believe that the best lawyers are shaped by real experience. From day one, our team members work directly on live transactions alongside our partners, gaining invaluable exposure to complex cross-border deals that would be difficult to find at larger firms.

We are a close-knit team that values intellectual curiosity, commercial awareness, and a genuine desire to deliver excellent results for our clients. If you are ambitious, eager to learn, and ready to take on responsibility early in your career, we want to hear from you.

Our office is located at 21/F, No. 8 Queen's Road Central — in the heart of Hong Kong's financial district — offering a dynamic and inspiring environment to grow your legal career.

Life at H.M. Chan & Co.

A Team That Works Hard & Celebrates Together

We believe a great firm is built on great people. Beyond the deals, we are a close-knit team that supports each other, gives back to the community, and celebrates our successes together.

If you're a junior or mid-level associate juggling too many IPOs, spending more time at the printers than with your family and getting confused about which weekly call you're actually on… this post is for YOU!

At H.M. Chan & Co., law is more than closing deals and billing clients. We believe in helping our lawyers rediscover why they chose this profession in the first place: to find joy in your work, create lasting relationships with clients and teammates and build a career that you can be proud of.

After a decade of achievements and a bold reintroduction, we're building a dynamic Asia platform and inviting ambitious talents to join our exciting growth journey.

What We Look For
  • Hong Kong-qualified lawyer
  • Experience in Hong Kong capital markets work
  • Strong drafting and negotiation skills with good business acumen, commercial sense and self-motivated
  • Detail-oriented with strong project management and execution skills
  • Ability to adapt to a fast-paced environment and work independently
  • Ability to interact effectively with multiple levels within an organisation and across various jurisdictions
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills in English and Mandarin
Why Choose Us
  • The opportunity to run IPO transactions with real support, supervision and actual learning
  • A culture that balances ambition with personal growth, so you can actually learn and thrive without the fear of burning out
  • Mentorship, not supervision, from partners who roll up their sleeves with you and genuinely care about your work, your well-being and your future
  • Opportunities to support clients from early fundraising, through M&A growth and post-listing matters to build lasting client relationships
  • Work on high-profile, cross-border transactions across Asia and worldwide
  • A diverse range of legal work beyond IPOs, spanning venture capital, pre-IPO investments, mergers and acquisitions, public takeovers, compliance and everything in between
  • Amazing clients, such as cutting-edge tech companies, AI startups, leading lifestyle brands and even limited-edition supercars

Our Programmes

We offer structured programmes designed to give aspiring lawyers a genuine taste of corporate legal practice at the highest level.

Internship Programme

We offer internships of up to 4 weeks to law students in their penultimate or final year who we believe have the necessary attributes to be a part of our team. As an intern, you will have the opportunity to assist with ongoing projects and transactions, work alongside our lawyers, and obtain an invaluable insight into our firm's operations and culture.

We look for candidates with excellent communication skills, an eagerness to learn, and a positive attitude. Prior experience in a legal or financial environment is an advantage but not a requirement.

Application Periods

Summer Internship: 1 December — 31 March

Winter Internship: 1 July — 30 September

Traineeship

As a trainee solicitor at H.M. Chan & Co., you will be exposed to a wide variety of transactions and gain a multitude of practical and technical experience, ranging from client management to drafting various documents. This will not only develop your skills and assist you in building a firm career foundation, but will also help you grow as part of our team.

Our trainees work directly with partners on live deals from day one, gaining hands-on experience that accelerates their development as corporate lawyers. We are committed to investing in the growth and development of our trainees.

How to Apply

Please send your application to hongkong@hmchan.com.hk with your cover letter, curriculum vitae, academic transcripts, and reference letters (if any). Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
