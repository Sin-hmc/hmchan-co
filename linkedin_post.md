# LinkedIn Post — China Deals of the Year 2025

---

**Proud to share that H.M. Chan & Co. has been recognised by China Business Law Journal in its prestigious China Deals of the Year 2025 awards!**

Our firm was honoured for our role as Hong Kong legal counsel to **JD.com, Inc.** in the landmark acquisition of **Kai Bo Food Supermarket** — one of Hong Kong's most iconic supermarket chains, with over 90 stores and thousands of employees.

This complex cross-border M&A transaction required navigating Hong Kong supermarket operation regulations, labour compliance, property matters, and global supply chain considerations — a true testament to our team's expertise in delivering precise, commercially-focused legal advice.

Please note that at the time of the transaction, our firm was operating under our former association with **Taylor Wessing**. The award was conferred under that name. We are proud that this recognition continues to reflect the excellence of our team.

We are grateful to our clients for their continued trust, and to our entire team for their dedication and hard work.
