# Design Research Notes

## Shihui Partners (www.shihuilaw.com)
- Clean minimal white background with full-width city skyline hero
- Awards section: uses actual award body logos (Chambers, Legal 500, ALB, asialaw, IFLR1000) in a 4-column grid
- Each award shows the logo + publication name + years in brackets
- Very clean, no clutter — just logos and text
- KEY INSIGHT: Use real award logos with years, not just text

## Titus (titus.com.hk)
- "Previous projects handled for" = scrolling carousel of CLIENT LOGOS (Airstar, Yardbird, RAZE, EASL, etc.)
- Very impactful — shows prestige through recognisable brand logos
- KEY INSIGHT: Use a client logo strip/carousel for "Selected Clients" section

## Mourant (www.mourant.com)
- Very premium, dark/moody photography
- Bold large typography: "In a fast-moving world, you need foresight, precision and a partner you trust."
- Clean news cards with category tags (NEWS / UPDATE)
- Minimal, lots of white space
- KEY INSIGHT: More white space, bolder hero copy, cleaner news cards

## Design Decisions for HMC Website
1. Awards section: Add real award body logos (ALB, IFLR1000, asialaw) with years
2. Homepage: Add a "Selected Clients" logo strip (JD.com, Alibaba, SK Group, TRATON, Lenovo, etc.)
3. News cards: Add category tags, cleaner layout, fix Read More links
4. Award feature: Use the real Deals of the Year 2025 logo image prominently
5. More white space throughout
