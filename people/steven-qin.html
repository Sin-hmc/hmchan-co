Biography

Steven Qin is a Counsel of H.M. Chan & Co.

With extensive experience in cross-border corporate transactions across Asia Pacific, Steven advises clients on mergers and acquisitions, public takeovers, capital markets, and corporate restructurings. His practice covers a wide range of sectors, including healthcare, consumer goods, technology and financial services.

Steven's clients include multinational corporations, private equity funds, and listed companies, whom he supports on strategic investments, IPOs, acquisitions, disposals and joint ventures.

Prior to joining H.M. Chan & Co., Steven gained valuable experience at a leading international law firm in Hong Kong, advising on complex cross-border transactions and regulatory compliance.

Steven is admitted as a solicitor in Hong Kong.
