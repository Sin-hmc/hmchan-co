Biography

Kevin Wong is a Legal Manager at H.M. Chan & Co. He has experience in capital markets, corporate transactions, and general corporate advisory work, with a focus on Hong Kong listed company matters and cross-border transactions.

Kevin works alongside the firm's partners and senior lawyers on a range of transactions including IPOs, secondary listings, and M&A deals. He brings strong attention to detail and project management skills to complex, multi-party transactions.

Practice Areas
  • Capital Markets — IPOs and equity financing
  • Mergers & Acquisitions
  • General Corporate — corporate governance and commercial contracts
  • Regulation & Compliance — listed company advisory
Qualifications
