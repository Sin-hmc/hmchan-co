# H.M. Chan & Co. Redesigned Website Sitemap

This document outlines the sitemap and content plan for the redesigned H.M. Chan & Co. website.

## 1. Main Navigation

- **Home**
- **About Us**
- **Practice Areas**
- **Our People**
- **News**
- **Careers**
- **Contact**

## 2. Page Content Outline

### Home

- **Hero Section:** Full-width hero image of Hong Kong skyline at night with the firm's logo and a tagline (e.g., "Premier Hong Kong Corporate Law Firm").
- **Firm Overview:** A brief introduction to H.M. Chan & Co., highlighting its expertise and client-centric approach.
- **Practice Areas:** A grid of cards, each representing a practice area with an icon and a short description. Each card links to the respective practice area page.
- **Our People:** A section featuring photos of Mark Chan and Avis Lau, with a link to the full "Our People" page.
- **Latest News:** A feed of the three most recent news articles.
- **Contact CTA:** A full-width section with a call-to-action to contact the firm.

### About Us

- **Firm History:** A detailed account of the firm's history, its founding, and its evolution.
- **Mission & Values:** A statement of the firm's mission, values, and commitment to its clients.
- **Why Choose Us:** Key differentiators and reasons why clients should choose H.M. Chan & Co.

### Practice Areas (Main Page)

- An overview of the firm's practice areas, with a grid of cards linking to individual practice area pages.

### Individual Practice Area Pages (e.g., Capital Markets)

- **Detailed Description:** A comprehensive description of the services offered in that practice area.
- **Our Expertise:** Examples of the firm's experience and expertise in that area.
- **Related News:** A list of news articles related to that practice area.

### Our People

- **Team Overview:** A brief introduction to the firm's legal team.
- **Team Profiles:** Individual profiles for each lawyer, including:
    - Professional photo
    - Title
    - Bio/Expertise
    - Education & Qualifications
    - Contact information

### News

- A blog-style layout with a list of news articles, each with a featured image, title, and excerpt.
- Articles will be categorized by practice area.

### Careers

- **Firm Culture:** A description of the firm's culture and what it's like to work at H.M. Chan & Co.
- **Internship Program:** Details about the internship program, including application periods.
- **Traineeship:** Information about the traineeship program.
- **How to Apply:** Instructions on how to apply for a position.

### Contact

- **Contact Form:** A simple and easy-to-use contact form.
- **Contact Details:** Address, phone number, fax number, and email address.
- **Map:** An embedded Google Map showing the firm's location.

### Footer

- **Disclaimers**
- **Privacy Policy**
- **LinkedIn Icon**
- **Copyright Information**
